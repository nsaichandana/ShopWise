# ShopWise - Project Vision

## Vision

ShopWise is a decision-support platform that helps users make confident purchase decisions by understanding their needs and providing personalized, explainable product recommendations.

Today, users spend hours browsing e-commerce websites, watching YouTube reviews, reading Reddit discussions, and asking AI tools for suggestions. Despite having access to large amounts of information, many users still feel uncertain about whether a product is actually suitable for their specific needs.

ShopWise aims to reduce this uncertainty.

Instead of asking users to understand technical specifications and compare products manually, ShopWise starts by understanding the user's requirements, priorities, and constraints. The platform then evaluates available products and recommends the most suitable options along with clear explanations of why those products were selected.

## Target Users

### Primary Users

* Students
* Young professionals
* First-time buyers

### Characteristics

* Know what they want to accomplish
* Do not know how to translate requirements into technical specifications
* Feel overwhelmed by too many product choices
* Want confidence before making a purchase

## Core Value Proposition

ShopWise does not focus on showing more products.

ShopWise focuses on helping users understand:

* What they actually need
* Which products fit those needs
* Why a product is recommended
* What trade-offs exist between different options

## Version 1 Scope

The first version of ShopWise will focus only on laptop recommendations.

The system will:

* Collect user requirements through guided questions
* Evaluate laptops against those requirements
* Calculate a match score
* Generate personalized explanations
* Recommend the most suitable products

The recommendation engine will be rule-based and fully explainable.

Machine learning and AI-driven recommendations are outside the scope of Version 1.
